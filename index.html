About Us

Established in 2005, CTR Design Group is known for architectural excellence. We are committed to providing outstanding design solutions, environmentally sensitive site plans and cost effective construction. We approach every project with a focus on aesthetics, functionality, and cost.
With over 20 years of experience, CTR team has been creating unique and innovative solutions for our clients.

If you can dream it, we can design it.

As principles of CTR Design Group, Ward and Robyn work together as a team ensuring their clients the best possible designs. Ward has many years of experience in the Custom and Production Home markets. His experience extends from Northern and Southern California to the Eastern United States. Efficient design and Value Engineering are incorporated into every design. While Robyn's background is in Multi-Family design and Interiors. Her experience in attached product design has earned recognition throughout the Southwest.
